Premium Bulk & Custom Branded CTIFREE Calculators

Tim Supply Enterprise delivers reliable, streamlined supply lines for corporate setups, universities, and secondary institutions. We specialize in the high-volume procurement of the premium CTIFREE ES991 Plus scientific series and professional desktop models. Built with advanced dual-power solar and battery engines, these units provide uninterrupted reliability in classrooms and examination halls alike.

Beyond standard supply, we provide custom-tailored identity solutions—allowing you to fuse your institutional pride or corporate branding directly onto every unit with crisp, high-end logo engraving and clean name printing.

Smart Institutional Solar & Backup Power Architecture

Tim Supply Enterprise engineers and supplies turnkey, modular solar energy arrays and smart battery backup systems specifically scaled for educational and corporate infrastructure. From powering critical ICT server rooms and university libraries to providing uninterrupted power for remote research field stations, we deploy commercial-grade hybrid systems. Our setups combine long-lifecycle Lithium Iron Phosphate ($LiFePO_4$) storage with high-efficiency PV arrays, ensuring zero downtime during power transitions without the recurring noise, fumes, and fueling costs of traditional diesel generators.

Key Supply Specifications:
Core Systems: 5kW to 50kW+ Hybrid Inverter Systems & Modular Scalable Battery Banks.
Engineering: Automated Transfer Switches (ATS) for instant millisecond power switching.
Target Scope: Computer labs, science faculties, administrative blocks, and corporate workspaces.

Institutional ICT Procurement & Digital Learning Hardware

Automotive Spare Parts & Fleet Logistics

OEM & Premium Aftermarket Vehicle Components

Tim Supply Enterprise specializes in high-reliability automotive parts procurement designed to keep commercial fleets, institutional transport networks, and agricultural machinery operating at peak efficiency. We source directly from trusted international manufacturing hubs—including South Korea, Germany, and China—to deliver both Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) parts and certified high-performance aftermarket components.

We understand that vehicle downtime directly impacts operational revenue. Our procurement system streamlines the sourcing of everything from critical internal engine components to heavy-duty suspension assemblies, electrical wiring harnesses, and filtration matrices. By verifying part compatibility and tracking manufacturing batches, we eliminate procurement errors and ensure your fleet assets remain on the move.

Key Supply Specifications
Powertrain & Mechanical: Comprehensive engine internal assemblies, transmission rebuild kits, heavy-duty brake systems, and precision suspension components.

Electrical & Control Systems: Alternators, starter motors, engine control units (ECUs), customized wiring looms, and advanced vehicle diagnostic sensors.

Fleet Maintenance Consumables: High-volume filtration packages (air, fuel, oil), heavy-duty lubricants, industrial belt drives, and severe-duty cooling system radiators.

Sourcing Integrity: Traceable OEM certification, strict component compatibility mapping, and prioritized express logistics for critical breakdown management.

Institutional Consumables & Operational Supply
